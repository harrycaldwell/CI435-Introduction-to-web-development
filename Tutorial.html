Welcome to my Tutorial page

On this page I will talk about the skills that I have developed this semester, specifically group working.

What is Group working?

"Group work refers to a collaborative learning environment where students work through problems and assessments together. Students are able to delegate roles and responsibilities, pool their knowledge and skills and receive support from one another." - Tophat definition

What have I learnt?

  1. Perspective helps - I found that while studying with someone else I am able to have more than one view on a subject allowing me to consider more options and solutions to a problem.
  2. Encouragement - Encouragement of peers and being Encouraged by peers should be used to push each other further to the goal that has been set.
  3. Setting Goals - Setting goals when working as a group helps create a sense of progress. This increases the motivation of the group and also provides a framework for what should be done.
  4. Problem Solving - Working in pairs allowed for the opportunity to work on my problem solving skills by listening to the Perspective of the person I was working with and being able to throw ideas at each other.

How affected me?

As of current I have not had any group working assigned to me, however this has not stopped the development of this skill as I have been able to work with my classmate, which helped me develop this website.

Before starting the semester studying was a difficult task, not for a lack of trying, I found that without being in group studies it was harder for me to focus and would require way more time then what I had. Studying in a group has allowed me to gain motivation and in fact allowed me to motivate the person I have been studying with, allowing us to push ourselves further and further.

I found that by working in a group I was also working on my social skills,it helped me work on communication.

Setting Goals has allowed me to take assignments step by step, and has allowed me to celebrate when completing parts of the assignment that I have set as a goal, it also has helped with my time management.

Working in a pair has allowed me to work using constructive criticism from my "study buddy" allowing me to change and add to the website and other work that I have been assigned.
